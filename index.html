Who We Are

Amigda Labs is a technology company focused on building AI-driven solutions that solve real-world problems—particularly in messaging, automation, scheduling, and payment processing. Our tools are built for speed, simplicity, and real business impact.

Founded in 2024 by Engr. Jose Jaime Felix II O. Garcia, Amigda Labs blends practical engineering with state-of-the-art AI. With over 6 years of experience in software development, Engr. Garcia brings deep technical insight into every solution we ship.

We specialize in agent-based systems using OpenAI Agents SDK, LangGraph, CrewAI, and AutoGen, and we work with technologies like LangChain, ChromaDB, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and fine-tuning open-source LLMs to build scalable, problem-first tools for business.

At Amigda, we believe in using AI not to impress—but to improve.
